# Local Keyword Research Template

Use this template to identify and track local keywords that drive targeted traffic. Copy the table below into a spreadsheet or document.

## Step 1: Brainstorm Seed Keywords
Start with core services/products + location modifiers (city, neighborhood, "near me").

| Seed Keyword | Location Modifier | Full Keyword Phrase |
|--------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| plumber      | Austin            | plumber Austin      |
| plumber      | near me           | plumber near me     |
| emergency plumber | Austin       | emergency plumber Austin |
| water heater repair | South Congress | water heater repair South Congress |

## Step 2: Expand with Keyword Tools
Use Google Keyword Planner, Ahrefs, SEMrush, or Ubersuggest to find variations. Add them to your tracking table.

## Step 3: Analyze Competition
For each keyword, note the difficulty and who ranks currently.

## Step 4: Map Keywords to Pages
Decide which page on your site will target each keyword (homepage, service page, blog post).

## Step 5: Group Keywords by Intent
- **Transactional**: "hire plumber Austin" → optimize service pages.
- **Informational**: "how to fix leaky faucet" → create blog posts.
- **Navigational**: "company name" → ensure homepage ranks.

## Step 6: Monitor & Update
Check rankings monthly and add new keyword opportunities as they arise.

**Bonus**: Use local keyword research to create location pages, blog topics, and Google Posts content.
