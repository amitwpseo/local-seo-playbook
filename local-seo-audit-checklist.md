# Local SEO Audit Checklist

Use this checklist to audit any local business website.

## Google Business Profile
- [ ] Is the GBP claimed and verified?
- [ ] Is NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistent with website?
- [ ] Are all categories selected correctly?
- [ ] Are photos added (logo, cover, interior/exterior)?
- [ ] Are posts published regularly?

## On-Page SEO
- [ ] Is the business name, address, and phone number in the website footer?
- [ ] Is there a dedicated "Location" or "Service Area" page?
- [ ] Are title tags and meta descriptions optimized for local keywords?
- [ ] Is LocalBusiness schema implemented?
- [ ] Are there customer testimonials with local references?

## Citations
- [ ] Is the business listed in top directories (Google, Bing, Apple Maps)?
- [ ] Are citations consistent across all platforms?
- [ ] Are there any duplicate listings?

## Reviews
- [ ] Does the business have at least 10 reviews on Google?
- [ ] Are reviews being responded to?
- [ ] Is review generation a regular process?

## Local Content
- [ ] Are there blog posts about local events or news?
- [ ] Are service pages optimized for "near me" searches?
- [ ] Does the website have a clear local focus?
